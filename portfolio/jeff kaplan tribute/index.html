Beginnings

Since highschool, Jeff has been a gamer. In college however, he pursued writing rather than a career in the video gaming industry, due to a lack of programming skills required for such an occupation. According to Rolling Stone, he worked for Universal Pictures as a writing intern, but "it didn't meet his romantic expectations about how writing for the movies should be". Following the failure of two novels, Jeff decided to give up writing . . . a momentous step that would eventually lead him to his future boss at his dream job.

"Tigole"

During his "playing & working" days, Jeff enjoyed the MMO EverQuest. Known as "Tigole", Jeff was recognized for his accomplishments & commentary on the "Legacy of Steel" guild website. He quickly caught the attention of guild members who happened to work at Blizzard. As time went on, the lead designer of Warcraft: III asked him to lunch. Jeff nearly declined, but ultimately accepted the invitation. That meal would go on to change his whole life.

Success

Jeff began his job on the Blizzard team in 2002 and remains there to this day. In regards to his personal life, he ended up meeting & marrying a guildmate from his EverQuest days. Today, not only is he known for his place on the team of the massively-successful shooter, Overwatch, but his Developer Update soundbites used in videos by popular Youtuber "dinoflask" have become something of a legend.
