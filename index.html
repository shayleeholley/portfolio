Shaylee Holley

A twenty-something web designer who games in her other free time.

I have been a designer for as long as I can remember. As a kid, one of my favorite hobbies was creating customized outfits for my American Girl paper dolls. In Jr. High, I sketched fairy dresses when I should have been listening to lectures.

Then I discovered web design. At first, I found free fonts to download & use for school & personal projects. Then, I started a blog and taught myself enough HTML & CSS to tweak Blogspot templates. Soon after that, I created my own blog backgrounds using digital scrapbooking kits & learned how to upload my favorite free fonts (because generic fonts are lame). One thing led to another and I quickly became hooked on digital designing.

Nine years & adulthood later, Bootstrap has completely changed my life.
