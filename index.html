About

What began as paper doll outfits, scrapbooks, and sketches of my dream home, evolved into attractive typography & classy color schemes combined in sassy stylesheets (see what I did there?). Though I currently work a full-time job that is entirely unrelated to web design, I create in my down time and continue to hone my skills in preparation for my dream career. When I'm not playing around in VSCode, you'll usually find me gaming, crying over my favorite anime, browsing Reddit, or laughing myself silly with my husband.
