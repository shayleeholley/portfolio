About

I have been a designer for as long as I can remember. As a kid, one of my favorite hobbies was creating customized outfits for my American Girl paper dolls. In Jr. High, I sketched fairy dresses & dream bedrooms when I should have been listening to lectures.

Then I discovered digital designing. At first, I found free fonts to download & use for school & personal projects. Then, I started a blog and taught myself enough HTML & CSS to tweak Blogspot templates. Soon after that, I created my own blog backgrounds using digital scrapbooking kits & learned how to upload my favorite free fonts (because generic fonts are lame). One thing led to another and I became immersed in the world of web designing.

As an adult, I now work a full-time job that is entirely unrelated to web design, and I program in my down time. When I'm not playing around in VSCode, you'll usually find me playing a Blizzard game with my husband.
